Browse Data Download PoC JSONL

What is this?

A unified dataset of edited diplomatic documents (letters, telegrams, despatches, memoranda) from official published series. The proof-of-concept includes a tiny sample from France (DDF), the UK (British Documents), and Germany (Grosse Politik).

What can I do?

Search by date and content, filter by country and document type, view the “from → to” header, and export the current selection as CSV or JSON for analysis.
